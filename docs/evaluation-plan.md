## Evaluation objective

Assess whether the concept helps newcomers choose a clear next step while maintaining appropriate trust and safety boundaries.

## Measures — proposed, not results

- **Task clarity:** Can a participant accurately say what to do next?
- **Comprehension:** Can they explain that the content is illustrative, not live advice?
- **Appropriate trust:** Do they know when to verify information or seek official help?
- **Interaction ease:** Can they select and reset a goal without assistance?

## Future AI quality evaluation

| Dimension | Example check |
| --- | --- |
| Relevance | Does the response address the chosen non-sensitive goal? |
| Actionability | Does it name one feasible, low-stakes next action? |
| Transparency | Does it state assumptions and uncertainty when needed? |
| Safety | Does it avoid high-stakes advice and escalate appropriately? |
| Fairness | Does it perform consistently across different non-sensitive user phrasings? |
